Roasted Chicken

Roasted chicken:

Roasted chicken is a classic dish that is both comforting and delicious. It involves cooking a whole chicken in the oven with a variety of seasonings and aromatics, resulting in tender, juicy meat with a crispy skin. Roasting a chicken may seem intimidating, but it is actually a simple process that can be easily mastered with a little practice. The key to a perfectly roasted chicken is to season it well, truss it tightly, and cook it at the right temperature for the right amount of time. The result is a beautifully browned bird that is perfect for any occasion, from a cozy family dinner to a holiday feast.

Roasted chicken can be served with a variety of sides, such as roasted vegetables, mashed potatoes, or a simple salad, making it a versatile dish that can be customized to suit any taste or occasion. Leftover roasted chicken can also be used in a variety of dishes, such as sandwiches, soups, or casseroles, making it a great option for meal planning and batch cooking. Whether you're a seasoned home cook or a beginner in the kitchen, roasted chicken is a classic and satisfying dish that is sure to please everyone at the table.

Ingredients:

These are the Ingredients needed to make roasted chicken:

    1 whole chicken (about 3-4 pounds)
  • Salt and black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 2-3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 lemon, cut into wedges
  • 1 head of garlic, cut in half crosswise
  • Fresh herbs like thyme, rosemary, and/or parsley (optional)

  3. In a small bowl, mix together 1 teaspoon of salt, 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper, 1/2 teaspoon of paprika, 1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder, and 1/2 teaspoon of onion powder.
  4. Rub the spice mixture all over the chicken, including inside the cavity.
  5. Drizzle 2-3 tablespoons of olive oil all over the chicken and use your hands to spread it evenly.
  6. Stuff the cavity of the chicken with lemon wedges and garlic halves. You can also add fresh herbs like thyme, rosemary, and/or parsley if you like.
  7. Place the chicken in a roasting pan, breast-side up.
  8. Roast the chicken for about 1 hour and 15 minutes, or until the internal temperature of the thickest part of the chicken reaches 165째F (74째C) when measured with a meat thermometer.
  9. Baste the chicken with its own juices or melted butter halfway through the cooking process to keep it moist and flavorful.
  10. Let the chicken rest for about 10-15 minutes before carving and serving.
