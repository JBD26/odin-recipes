Tacos

Tacos:

Tacos are a traditional Mexican dish that has become a popular food all over the world. They consist of a corn or flour tortilla filled with various ingredients such as meat, beans, cheese, vegetables, and salsa. Tacos can be served soft or crispy and can be topped with guacamole, sour cream, and other condiments. Tacos are a versatile food that can be customized to suit individual tastes. They can be filled with different types of meat, such as chicken, beef, or pork, or made vegetarian by using beans or grilled vegetables. Tacos can be a quick and easy meal, or an elaborate dish that takes time to prepare. They're great for lunch or dinner, and can be enjoyed alone or as part of a larger Mexican-inspired feast. Whether you prefer spicy or mild flavors, tacos are a delicious and satisfying food that's enjoyed by people all over the world.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 12 small corn or flour tortillas
  • Toppings: shredded lettuce, chopped tomatoes, shredded cheese, salsa, sour cream, guacamole

Steps:

  1. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, brown the ground beef, breaking it up with a wooden spoon. Add the diced onion and minced garlic and cook until the onion is soft and the beef is browned.
  2. Add the chili powder, ground cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper to the beef mixture, and stir well to combine.
  3. Reduce the heat to medium-low and let the beef mixture simmer for 5-10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
  4. While the beef is simmering, heat the tortillas in a separate skillet or in the microwave according to package instructions.
  5. To assemble the tacos, spoon some of the beef mixture onto each tortilla, and top with shredded lettuce, chopped tomatoes, shredded cheese, salsa, sour cream, and/or guacamole, as desired.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious beef tacos!
